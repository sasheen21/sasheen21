The Civil War that took place in the year, 1983, was a dark day for our beloved nation.
Hence, it was also called "Black July". Many people have lost their lives that day. But, it is
not only the lives of countless innocent people that were in ruins but also, the long-term bond that existed
between the Sinhalese and the Tamil community.

The attacks from both the North and the South have reigned for 30 long years, as a result of ethnic violence. This was the
pure definition of "HELL".
 All of that ended in 2009 when the president, at that time, declared the end of the 30 year civil war. The war may be over but, terror,
fear and hatred
towards the other race is a conflict that still remains unsolved up to this day.
